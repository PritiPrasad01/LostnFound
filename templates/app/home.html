Lost & Found

Our goal is to return the owners their lost items.Lost and found management system, manage and track the lost and found items. This website is developed for the students, faculty members and visitors of the universities yet anyone is able to use it.

Many things are lost in the day to day life. Most of the people found or lost things. For a small reason, we contact to other people. People are being responsible and know their duties. So this system will help users to post the things they found and lost things.

Your personal information will not be disclosed with anyone.

Step-By-Step Process

  • Log into your account or Sign Up if you are a new user.
  • Enter lost or found item details (e.g., item lost or found, category, photos, date, time, etc).
  • Review details entered and submit the lost or found item report.
  • Receive notifications when someone finds your item or the owner wants to connect with you.
  • Happy Connecting
