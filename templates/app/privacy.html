Privacy Policy


At LostnFound, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and how we protect your information.
Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address if you choose to provide it to us when filling out a contact form or submitting a lost/found item report.
  • Website Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit and the actions you take on our site.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to improve your experience on our website and to collect usage information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you do not wish to allow their use.

How We Use Your Information ?

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries and requests.
  • Facilitating lost and found item reports.
  • Improving our website and services.
  • Sending promotional emails and updates about our services (if you opt-in to receive these communications).

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. We store your information on secure servers and use encryption and other security measures to protect your data.

We may disclose your information if required by law or if necessary to protect our legal rights.
